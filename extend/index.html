Chapter 1, Extend Your Knowledge

Stephen Davis. September 4, 2022.

Guideline 1.1 explains text alternatives for any non-text content. This includes controls and inputs, time-based media, tests and exercises, sensory experiences, CAPTCHA, and text that is purely for decoration,only for visual formatting, or is not visible to users.

Guideline 1.2 covers aspects of time-based media. This includes both audio and video, as well as supporting aspects such as captions and sign language interpretation.

Guideline 1.3 talks about how content that can be presented in different ways. Consideration includes sequencing, sensory characteristics, orientation, and purpose.

Guideline 1.4 goes over making content distinguishable. This includes usage of colors, contrast, audio control, text size, and images of text.
